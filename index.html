What is this website about?

This website is about an island just of the coast off Australia where it is home to many species of wildlife both native and foreign, our little island is home to the most amazing cultures and if you come and visit you will be able to taste the various food dishes we have to offer. We have fun activities for you to do with your family like hiking, scooba diving and a nature walk.
